De Anza Malaysian And Singaporean Association

Malaysians and Singaporeans in De Anza

WHO WE ARE

The De Anza MASA Club is a student-led body that unites Malaysian and Singaporean students and those interested in the culture across campus. It fosters leadership, professionalism, and meaningful connections while supporting the personal and professional growth of students through collaboration, networking, and community-building initiatives.

Our Organizational Pillars

Advocacy & Service

We vow to be the platform where students can voice out their opinions and concerns, ensuring that no one in our De Anza family is left behind or unheard.

Culture & Lifestyle

As the faces of Malaysia on campus, we display Malaysian hospitality with pride. We host cultural festivals, food gatherings, and sports events to promote our heritage.

Career Development

We strive to help the development of future leaders by finding opportunities that benefit our members through workshops, study groups, and networking with alumni.
